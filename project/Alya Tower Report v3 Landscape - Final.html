◇ Project Overview

Alya Tower is a 30-floor luxury apartment building planned for the North Corniche in Jeddah — one of the city's most prestigious waterfront locations. The tower will deliver 65 premium residences, expanded from an earlier plan of 30 units to better reflect the site's full potential and market demand.

The land is owned by HH Prince Mohammed bin Abdullah Al Saud. Tashyid Urban Development is leading the development on his behalf.

We are in Phase 1 (April – July 2026), covering architectural design and funding. Construction begins January 2027, completing by May 2029.
